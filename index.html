Our Story

Discover the unique flavours of South Africa with our premium range of Biltong and Droewors. Our Biltong, a succulent, air-dried meat delicacy, is crafted using traditional South African methods, ensuring an authentic taste experience. Each piece of beef biltong is seasoned to perfection, melding spices with the rich, natural flavours of high-quality British beef. Similarly, our Droewors, a delectably spiced dried sausage, offers a robust and satisfying snack, perfect for any time of the day. Whether you're craving a savoury treat or looking for the ideal accompaniment to your gatherings, our Biltong and Droewors are just a click away.
